Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data DollarDojo collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

DollarDojo collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — if you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
  • App data — the savings amounts, debt amounts, and training logs you manually enter into the app.
  • Game state — your XP, belt level, streak, badges, and goals.
  • Purchase information — subscription status is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.

If you use DollarDojo as a guest (no account), all data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide and sync your DollarDojo experience across devices
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services

DollarDojo uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Your account data and game state are stored on Supabase's servers. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase verification. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — all payments are processed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authenticated user data is stored securely on Supabase with row-level security enabled, meaning each user can only access their own data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Guest data is stored only on your local device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@dollardojo.app

7. Children's Privacy

DollarDojo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
